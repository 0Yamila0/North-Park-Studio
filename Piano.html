Piano

Here is the page about our piano lessons. Here we will begin to teach you about the piano and music theory or build off what you already know. Piano Lessons consist of one 50-minute lesson per week in a private or group setting. Group lessons have anywhere between 2-6 students at a time. Both group and private lessons include technology-based theory and the opportunity to perform in a casual atmosphere for other students, as well as a part of recitals and community events. At the end of the course, students will play a piece that they have learned on our performance piano in a concert. We welcome all ages beginning at 4 years old. And adults . . . you are never too old to experience the joy of playing the piano!




All lessons are currently based on a flat monthly fee. Those fees are as follows:

Private lessons $122 per month
Group lessons $87 per month


There is a discount for the second student in a family or the second class in which a student is enrolled.

Additional Private $110 per month
Additional Group $78 per month


Important: There will be a total of 47 lessons and 12 makeup lessons offered over a 12-month period. However, your cost is based on only 44 lessons. Some months may have five lessons - others may have only three. The monthly fee will remain fixed. Your cost covers registration, books and recitals. There are no hidden fees! All makeup lessons are in group format (divided by age) and meet on Saturday once a month.
