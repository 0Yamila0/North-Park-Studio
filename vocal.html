Vocal

Private and Group Voice Lessons are available for children ages 5 through adults. Students will practice songs in a variety of music genres and be allowed to focus on personal music goals while learning good vocal habits. Students will also learn basic music theory concepts. Opportunities for performance will be made available throughout the semester. Weekly lessons are 50 minutes in length.




All lessons are currently based on a flat monthly fee. Those fees are as follows:

Private lessons $122 per month
Group lessons $87 per month


There is a discount for the second student in a family or the second class in which a student is enrolled.

Additional Private $110 per month
Additional Group $78 per month


Important: There will be a total of 47 lessons and 12 makeup lessons offered over a 12-month period. However, your cost is based on only 44 lessons. Some months may have five lessons - others may have only three. The monthly fee will remain fixed. Your cost covers registration, books and recitals. There are no hidden fees! All makeup lessons are in group format (divided by age) and meet on Saturday once a month.


You will not recieve a monthy invoice. Please set uppayments to autopay or pay for multiple months in advance. Monthly fees are due on the 15th of the month preceding the lessons to keep your preferred time slot. For example, the fee for February lessons is due on January 15th. you may pay with cash, check, credit card, or Paypal. There will be a 3% processing fee for Paypal and credit card payments.
