Theater Arts

Private and Group Classes in Theatre Arts will be offered, in cooperation with Jackson Youth Acting Academy, for ages 4-17. Classes will meet for one hour each week. These classes will cover all aspects of acting and stage production. Parents are encouraged to help with sets and costumes. Multiple opportunities for performance will be made available during the semester, including a full production to be performed at the end of the semester. Additional rehearsals outside of class times are often necessary leading up to productions and are included at no extra charge. Private and group instruction for adults is also available covering topics such as public speaking, character development, accents, stage makeup and stage production.



All lessons are currently based on a flat monthly fee. Those fees are as follows:

Private lessons $122 per month
Group lessons $87 per month


There is a discount for the second student in a family or the second class in which a student is enrolled.

Additional Private $110 per month
Additional Group $78 per month


Important : There will be a total of 47 lessons and 12 makeup lessons offered over a 12-month period. However, your cost is based on only 44 lessons. Some months may have five lessons - others may have only three. The monthly fee will remain fixed. Your cost covers registration, books and recitals. There are no hidden fees! All makeup lessons are in group format (divided by age) and meet on Saturday once a month.
