Welcome

Welcome to our website for Northpark Studio of the Arts! Here is where we can hopefully give you plenty of information about what we offer. Come by our studio located in the Shoppes at Northpark for a tour of the studio and more information. We offer piano, vocal, and theater lessons. We are open most days of the week and offer private or group lessons. We have a decent number of students and we are constantly growing. If you aren't interested in a whole semester of lessons, we offer one-week summer camps. We would be more than happy to have you with us!

Our Studio

Our studio is fully equipped with new equipment, including a Yamaha AvanteGrand Piano and six Yamaha Clavinova series keyboards for group lessons. We also use technology to facilitate learning in music and theory with three computer stations and three iPad stations. We have two private practice rooms which can be used for private lessons or rented for student practice sessions. Having fun with music and drama is also encouraged through games and friendly competition. Performance opportunities are very important and include group lesson concerts, end-of-semester recitals, as well as community event participation.
