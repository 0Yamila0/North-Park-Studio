Summer Camps




Camps will be held Monday - Friday 1pm - 4pm. Registration is open to children who have completed kindergarten through sixth grade. Every week of camp will be different! You may register for 1 week or all of them! Students completing grades 7 & above can be scheduled as camp assistants in any of our 1-week camps for a cost of $50 to cover supplies, etc. Space is very limited. Pick up your registration form at the studio. There is a $25 per week non-refundable deposit due to hold your spot. Deposit will be credited to your camp fee.
