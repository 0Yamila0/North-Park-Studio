Summer Camps




Camps will be held Monday - Friday 1pm - 4pm. Registration is open to children who have completed kindergarten through sixth grade. Every week of camp will be different! You may register for 1 week or all of them! Students completing grades 7 & above can be scheduled as camp assistants in any of our 1-week camps for a cost of $50 to cover supplies, etc. Space is very limited. Pick up your registration form at the studio. There is a $25 per week non-refundable deposit due to hold your spot. Deposit will be credited to your camp fee. Sign up here!

Our Story

The owner of Northpark Studio, Mrs. Deborah, grew up in Jackson, TN. Her dad was a preacher and her mom played the piano, but she learned from her piano teachers, and took voice lessons. She loved the classes and they were the highlight of her day. In college, she discovered her loved for theatre and managed to get her degree in English communications with a lot of theatre, piano, and voice classes. Once she got married, she did not quit her dreams of the arts. She began to teach a couple kids piano and voice lessons.


When she and her husband moved to Little Rock, Arkansas she opened a studio there which grew to contain 118 students. She had the idea of opening that studio when she taught at a private school in Little Rock. She opened this studio in July 18, 2018 once she got her business license. The studio first started with only 17 kids, but the studio has grown like crazy! And now there are 59 students are participating in the classes. She wants this studio to grow and become a loving community for the arts. “I hope all of you find things to do with your lives that you can enjoy as much as I enjoy this, cause it’s pretty cool.”
